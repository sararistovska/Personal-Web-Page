About Me

I was born and raised in Tetovo, Macedonia — a city that shaped both my calm side and my curiosity. I've always enjoyed moments that feel simple but meaningful — whether it’s a quiet drive, a sunset walk, or discovering new places that leave a lasting impression.

Over time, I’ve found myself drawn to things that blend creativity with clarity. I like when something looks good, but also has purpose. Whether it’s designing, painting, or just organizing my thoughts into something visual, I enjoy making things that reflect who I am.

This space is just that — a place to collect and share the things I love, the paths I’m on, and the little details that make life feel personal.
